!Your action list (in priority order)

Do these 3 first — they matter most

  • Claim your Google Business Profile at business.google.com — complete every field, pick the category "Eyebrow bar / Beauty salon", and add lots of photos. (This is the #1 driver of being found locally.)
  • Start collecting Google reviews — ask every happy client; aim for a few each week. Reply to all of them. (Reviews are the biggest ranking booster.)
  • Confirm the real website address is live (not a temporary link), then verify it in Google Search Console and submit the sitemap. (Your developer can do the technical part.)

Then, when you have time:

  • Switch on automatic Google reviews (get a Google API key — Reviews guide, Section 2 — and give it to your developer).
  • Make sure your name, address & phone are written identically on your website, Google, and Facebook.
  • List your business on Waze, Apple Maps, and Philippine beauty directories (same details everywhere).
  • Post on your Google Business Profile weekly (promos, before/afters).
One honest note about "#1" No website alone can guarantee the #1 spot — Google decides it based mostly on your Business Profile and your reviews. The good news: the website now does its full part, and the 3 priority steps above are squarely within your control. Stay consistent for a few months and the rankings climb. It's a marathon, not a sprint.
Need help? Hand the technical items (Google key, domain, verification) to your website developer — the detailed files are saved in the project's docs folder. Everything else on the checklist is yours, and none of it requires code.
