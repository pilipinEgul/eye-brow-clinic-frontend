1How local ranking actually works

"#1" is really two contests — you want both:

Map Pack
The top-3 map results. Driven mostly by your Google Business Profile, reviews and proximity. This is where most local clicks go — and it's mostly owner-driven.
Organic links
The normal blue results. Driven by the website — content, town pages, schema, speed, backlinks. This is where the build work pays off.

Google weighs three things for local: Relevance (do you match the search?), Distance (how near is the searcher?), and Prominence (how well-known & reviewed are you?).

The honest timeline Local SEO moves in weeks to months, not days. Top-3 in Imus is realistically ~1–3 months of steady work; winning competitive terms across all of Cavite is a 6–12 month effort built on consistent reviews and content. No single change flips a switch — this is a compounding game.

3The 90-day plan

Phase 0 — Foundations (Week 1)

  • Owner Claim & verify your Google Business Profile; fill in every field.
  • Owner Verify the site in Google Search Console & submit the sitemap.
  • Dev Set the real domain (NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL); add OG image, favicon, analytics.

Phase 1 — Profile & relevance (Weeks 2–4)

  • Owner Set the right primary category ("Eyebrow bar" / "Beauty salon") + secondary categories (Facial spa, Skin care clinic, Laser hair removal). Categories are a top ranking factor.
  • Owner Add your services & 20+ photos; set service area to all 6 towns.
  • Owner Post weekly GBP updates (promos, before/afters).
  • Owner Make NAP (Name, Address, Phone) identical on site, Google & Facebook.

Phase 2 — Reviews engine (Weeks 2–12, ongoing)

  • Owner Ask every happy client for a Google review (use the site's "Write a Google review" button/link).
  • Owner Aim for a steady 4–8 reviews/month — velocity & recency beat a one-time burst.
  • Owner Reply to every review; naturally mention the treatment & town.

Phase 3 — Citations & directories (Weeks 4–8)

  • Owner List the business with identical NAP on Facebook, Instagram, Waze, Apple Maps, Foursquare, and PH beauty/booking directories.

Phase 4 — Content & backlinks (Weeks 6–12+)

  • Dev Add a simple blog/journal for treatment & local posts (long-term organic engine).
  • Owner Earn local backlinks — partner salons, local bloggers/influencers, town pages, supplier listings, press.
  • Owner Add real before/after photos + town-specific reviews to each area page.

5How to measure progress

  • Search Console → Performance — track position per search term & per town page over time.
  • Map Pack check — search "brows in <town>" from that town; note your map position monthly.
  • Business Profile Insights — calls, direction requests, discovery searches.
  • Reviews — count & average trend (the strongest leading indicator of Map Pack rank).
Monthly rhythm Once a month: check Search Console positions, count new reviews, see which town pages are climbing — then double down on what's working.

6The 5 things that matter most

  1. Google Business Profile — complete, correct category, active.
  2. Reviews — steady stream, replied to, every month.
  3. Town pages (done) — kept fresh with real photos.
  4. NAP consistency — same details everywhere online.
  5. Patience + consistency — show up monthly; rankings compound.
